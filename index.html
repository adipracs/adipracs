About adidrawsart

adidrawsart is a young artist exploring how images can tell stories — from pencil sketches in a notebook to digital experiments on an iPad.

The interest sits at the place where art meets technology and science: how a single drawing can explain something complex, and how new tools are changing what artists can make.

This site is where the work lives. It will grow over time.
